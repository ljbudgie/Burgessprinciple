# DSAR WARRANT SPECIFIC

## Purpose
This document outlines the specific requirements and procedures for handling Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR) and warrants.

## Scope
This applies to all personnel involved in the processing of DSARs and warrants.

## Procedures
1. **Request Verification**  
   Ensure the identity of the requester is verified before processing.

2. **Data Retrieval**  
   Locate the data subject's information in accordance with company policies.

3. **Compliance**  
   Follow all legal and regulatory requirements when fulfilling requests.

## Record Keeping
Maintain records of all requests and responses for accountability and legal compliance.
